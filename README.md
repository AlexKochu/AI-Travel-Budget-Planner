AI-Travel-Budget-Planner 
- is a smart and intuitive application designed to assist users in effectively planning and managing their travel-related expenses. The platform combines traditional budgeting tools with artificial intelligence to provide personalized financial insights and support responsible spending while traveling.

Features
Real-time Budget Overview: Displays total income, total expenses, and the remaining budget in a clear and organized manner.

Categorized Spending: Automatically tracks and categorizes expenses such as housing, food, transportation, and entertainment.

Goal Setting and Tracking: Enables users to set financial goals, such as saving for emergencies or vacations, and monitor their progress.

AI Budget Assistant: Offers intelligent suggestions and guidance to help users adjust their budgets and make informed financial decisions.

Calendar and Timeline Integration: Helps users manage daily spending limits and view financial activity across the month.

Analytics and Reporting: Provides insights into spending habits and budget performance through visual and statistical analysis.

Technology Stack
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Backend: Node.js and Express (if applicable)

AI Integration: GPT-based logic for assistant functionality

Hosting Platform: Netlify

How to Use
Access the AI-Travel-Budget-Planner dashboard through the hosted application.

Enter your monthly travel income and input your expenses across relevant categories.

Define financial goals to save or manage travel costs.

Review insights and suggestions provided by the AI assistant.

Monitor your spending habits and make data-informed decisions based on analytics.

Project Objective
The goal of this project is to simplify and enhance the process of travel budgeting through the integration of artificial intelligence. By offering a centralized platform for financial planning, the AI-Travel-Budget-Planner empowers users to manage their travel expenses efficiently, avoid overspending, and achieve their financial objectives while on the move.
